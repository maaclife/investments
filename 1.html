investment

[ in- vest -m uh nt ] SHOW IPA / ɪnˈvɛst mənt / PHONETIC RESPELLING SEE SYNONYMS FOR investment ON THESAURUS.COM

noun

the investing of money or capital in order to gain profitable returns, as interest, income, or appreciation in value. a particular instance or mode of investing . a thing invested in, as a business, a quantity of shares of stock, etc. something that is invested ; sum invested . the act or fact of investing or state of being invested , as with a garment. a devoting, using, or giving of time, talent, emotional energy, etc., as for a purpose or to achieve something: His investment in the project included more time than he cared to remember. Biology . any covering, coating, outer layer, or integument, as of an animal or vegetable. the act of investing with a quality, attribute, etc. investiture with an office, dignity, or right. a siege or blockade; the surrounding of a place with military forces or works, as in besieging. Also called investment compound. Metallurgy . a refractory material applied in a plastic state to a pattern to make a mold. Archaic . a garment or vestment. SEE MORE SEE LESS
